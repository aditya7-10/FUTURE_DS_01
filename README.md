Project Overview :-
Developed an interactive Superstore Sales Dashboard in Power BI to analyze sales performance and business trends. Created DAX measures for key KPIs such as Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Total Quantity, Profit Margin, Average Order Value, and Total Customers. Designed an interactive dashboard with slicers, KPI cards, line charts, bar charts, a map, and a donut chart to provide insights into sales by category, region, customer, and segment. The dashboard enables users to monitor performance, identify top-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions.
